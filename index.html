Introduction and Objective of Field Project

Introduction: This project report details the experience and findings from a field visit to a Samsung store.Samsung is a leading electronics brand with a strong retail presence. The purpose of this visit was to observe store layout, product range, customer interaction, and to gather data for creating a model that represents store processes.

Objective: The objective of this project is to understand how a Samsung store operates, identify key processes and components, and develop a system that can digitally represent store functionalities using a database, ER diagrams, and a basic user interface design.

Entity-Relationship (ER) Diagram

Entities:

  1. Customer: Stores customer data like Customer ID, Name, Contact.
  2. Product: Contains details about products, including Product ID, Name, Category, Price, and Stock.
  3. Transaction: Represents sales transactions, with fields like Transaction ID, Date, Customer ID, Product ID, Quantity, and Total Price.
  4. Staff: Contains details of store employees with fields such as Staff ID, Name, and Position.

Relationships:

  1. Customer - Transaction: A customer can make multiple transactions.
  2. Product - Transaction: A transaction involves one or multiple products.
  3. Staff - Transaction: A staff member processes the transaction.

Stakeholders and Their Tasks

Customers
Tasks: Browse products, purchase items, provide feedback, and make inquiries.

Product
Tasks: Represent details like name, category, price, and stock availability,Link products to transactions for customer purchases and Update stock levels after transactions.

Transaction
Tasks: Record each purchase made by customers, Link customers to the products they buy and Calculate and store total price for each transaction.

Staff
Tasks: Assist customers in making informed purchase decisions, Manage and update product inventory, Handle the recording and verification of transactions and Address customer inquiries or issues.

Store Visit: Question & Answer Series

1: How long has the store been in operations?

A: The store was established in January 2015 and has been serving customers since then.

2: What are the most popular products in this store?

A: The most popular products are the latest Samsung smartphones and smart TVs.

3: What services does the store provide besides product sales?

A: Apart from selling products, the store offers product demonstrations, warranty services, and technical support.

4: What are the business hours?

A: From 10:30am to 9:30 pm daily.

5: Do you use any Management Software?

A: Yes, it is provided by Samsung for Management and Security purpose.

6: Do you Accept mobile payments(e.g., apple pay,Google pay,etc)?

A: Yes, Accept all kinds of payments.

7: Do you have an e-commerce website?

A: No, Not yet but have plans to launch in future

8: What training programs do you offer for employees?

A: Yes, We offer basic 3monts training program.

9: How does the store train its staff on new products?

A: Staff undergo regular training sessions whenever new products are launched to ensure they have up-to-date knowledge for assisting customers.

10: What are the future plans for the store?

A: The store plans to expand its service offerings, introduce more smart home products, and enhance the online shopping experience.

Technology and Tools Used

The store utilizes advanced inventory management systems to keep track of stock in real-time. Additionally, there are digital displays that provide product details and promotions, as well as self-service kiosks where customers can check prices and availability. This technology enhances customer experience and streamlines store operations.
